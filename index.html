NAMING CULTURE AMONG THE AKAN PEOPLE

Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week in which they were born and the order in which they were born. The "day names" have further meaning in regards to the soul and character of the person. Most Ghanians have at leastone name from this system, even if they also have an English or Christian name.
